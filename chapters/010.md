## 10 - The Fire That Awakens

> *Jesus said, "I have cast fire upon the world, and see, I am guarding it until it blazes."*

Fire is a powerful image. It can warm, illuminate, transform, and refine. It can turn darkness into light and reveal what could not be seen before. When Jesus speaks of casting fire upon the world, we might imagine not a fire of destruction, but a fire of awakening—a living flame that burns within the human heart.

Many of us have experienced moments when something deep within suddenly came alive. A truth we could no longer ignore. A longing that refused to remain buried. A glimpse of beauty that changed how we saw the world. These moments can feel like sparks landing in dry grass. Something begins to burn that was not burning before.

The spiritual life often starts quietly. A question emerges. A sense of restlessness appears. A desire for deeper meaning grows stronger. We may be tempted to dismiss these stirrings, yet they are often the beginnings of transformation. The fire arrives before the blaze. The awakening begins long before we understand where it is leading.

This fire does not merely give comfort; it also reveals. It shines into corners we would rather avoid. It illuminates habits, fears, and illusions that have kept us asleep. Yet its purpose is not condemnation. Fire clears away what no longer serves life so that something truer can emerge. What feels uncomfortable may actually be part of our becoming.

Jesus says that he is guarding the fire until it blazes. There is tenderness hidden in that image. The flame is not abandoned. It is watched over. Protected. Nourished. The sacred work unfolding within you does not depend entirely on your own effort. There is a deeper wisdom tending the process, patiently encouraging the spark toward fullness.

Perhaps the invitation today is simply to notice the fire already present. What longing is alive within you? What truth continues to call your attention? What quiet spark refuses to go out? Rather than fearing it, draw near. The flame that awakens the soul is also the flame that lights the way home.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Tend the fire within*
>
> Exhale: *Let it shine with love*

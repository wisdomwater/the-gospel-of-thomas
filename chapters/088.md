## 88 - The Gifts Already Given

> *Jesus said, "The angels and the prophets will come to you and give to you those things you (already) have. And you too, give them those things which you have, and say to yourselves, 'When will they come and take what is theirs?'"*

This saying carries the feel of a riddle. Angels and prophets arrive to give us what we already possess. How can someone give us something that is already ours? The paradox invites us to look beneath the surface and consider the nature of spiritual awakening.

Often, the deepest truths are not things we acquire but things we remember. A wise teacher does not place wisdom into us like an object into an empty container. Rather, they help us recognize what has been quietly present all along. They point toward a treasure we overlooked, a light we forgot, a truth hidden in plain sight.

Many spiritual experiences unfold in this way. A book, a conversation, a prayer, or a moment of silence suddenly reveals something that feels both new and strangely familiar. We do not merely learn it; we recognize it. The soul responds with a sense of homecoming. Something deep within us whispers, "I knew this before I knew it."

Jesus then turns the image around. Not only do we receive; we also give. We are invited to share what we have been given, even while recognizing that these gifts do not ultimately belong to us. Love flows through us, wisdom flows through us, compassion flows through us. We become stewards rather than owners of what is sacred.

This perspective brings a certain lightness to the spiritual life. We do not need to grasp tightly at our insights, our experiences, or our understanding. We receive them gratefully, share them generously, and trust their source. What comes from God remains God's, even as it blesses the world through us.

Perhaps the invitation today is to notice what has already been placed within you. What wisdom have you overlooked? What grace have you taken for granted? What gifts are quietly waiting to be shared? The Kingdom is not always found by acquiring more. Sometimes it is discovered by awakening to the abundance that has been present from the beginning.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Awaken what is already within*
>
> Exhale: *May it flow through me*

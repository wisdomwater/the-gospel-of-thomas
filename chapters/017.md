## 17 - Beyond What Can Be Imagined

> *Jesus said, "I shall give you what no eye has seen and what no ear has heard and what no hand has touched and what has never occurred to the human mind."*

This saying opens a window onto mystery. Jesus speaks of a gift that cannot be seen, heard, touched, or even imagined. It is as though he is pointing beyond the boundaries of ordinary experience, toward something so vast and beautiful that our usual ways of knowing cannot contain it.

Much of life is spent pursuing what we can perceive. We seek visible success, tangible security, familiar comforts, and predictable outcomes. The mind naturally works with what it already knows, arranging old experiences into future expectations. Yet the deepest realities often arrive from beyond those familiar patterns. Grace enters through doors we did not know existed.

There are moments when life surprises us in this way. A wound becomes a source of compassion. A season of loss opens into unexpected wisdom. A chance encounter changes the direction of our journey. Looking back, we realize that what arrived was far greater than anything we would have planned for ourselves. The heart discovers possibilities the mind could never have anticipated.

The spiritual journey is filled with such surprises. We begin by searching for answers, but often we are being invited into something deeper than answers. We are being invited into relationship, presence, and transformation. The greatest gifts of the Spirit are not objects we possess but ways of seeing, ways of loving, and ways of being that gradually awaken within us.

This saying also encourages humility. We do not need to understand everything before we can trust. The mind is a wonderful servant, but it cannot map the entire landscape of the soul. There are dimensions of divine love that can only be known through experience. They are discovered not through grasping, but through openness.

Today, allow yourself to release the need to predict what comes next. The future may contain blessings your imagination has never conceived. The sacred often arrives quietly, hidden within ordinary moments, carrying gifts beyond expectation. Remain attentive. Remain receptive. The next unfolding of your journey may be more beautiful than anything you currently know how to imagine.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Open my heart*
>
